Academic Leadership

Prof. Dr. Jasmine Sheela

Principal, M.Sc. (N), Ph.D.

Principal's Profile

Prof. Dr. Jasmine Sheela embarked on her remarkable nursing journey as a Registered Nurse and Midwife (RN RM) from 1990 to 1994 at a reputed multispecialty hospital in Chennai. She pursued her higher education at The Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, earning her Post Certificate B.Sc. Nursing (P.C.B.Sc.) in 2001 and her M.Sc. in Paediatric Nursing in 2005.

With 8 years of clinical expertise and 20 years of teaching experience, Dr. Jasmine Sheela has been an inspiring leader, serving as the Principal of Mount Zion College of Nursing for the past 14 years. She attained her Ph.D. in Nursing in 2018 from The Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, Chennai.

Her areas of expertise include curriculum planning, policy development, and integrating innovative teaching-learning methodologies. Under her guidance, MZCN has consistently excelled in fostering professional growth. She has organized numerous national and state-level conferences and has enriched the field of nursing through various scientific publications in peer-reviewed journals.

Prof. Dr. Jerin Kumar J

Vice Principal, M.Sc.(N), Ph.D.

Vice Principal's Profile

Prof. Dr. Jerin Kumar J serves as the Vice Principal of Mount Zion College of Nursing, Pudukkottai. With over 15 years of experience in teaching, he holds a Ph.D. in Nursing from The Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, along with an M.Sc. in Nursing and an M.Sc. in Counseling and Psychotherapy.

His expertise spans mental health, counseling, and academic leadership. He serves as a Doctoral Committee Expert at Chettinad Academy of Research and Education and has held key positions in reputed institutions like AIIMS Patna and Karunya Institute. He is actively involved in organizing workshops and training programs focused on mental health and innovative nursing practices.

As Vice Principal, Dr. Jerin Kumar J is committed to promoting quality education, research, and the overall growth of students, ensuring they excel in their nursing careers as balanced healthcare professionals.
