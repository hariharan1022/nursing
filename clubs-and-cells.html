1. Curriculum Committee

The Curriculum Committee is responsible for designing, evaluating, and updating the academic programs to ensure they meet the educational and professional requirements of nursing students.

Objectives:

  • Develop a Comprehensive Curriculum integrating theory, practice, and ethical values.
  • Align with Regulatory Standards set by INC, TNNMC, and MGR University.
  • Incorporate Evidence-Based Practices reflecting advancements in medical science.
  • Promote Holistic Education including mental health and cultural competence.
  • Facilitate Skill Development through clinical rotations and simulations.
  • Encourage Student Feedback to enhance curriculum effectiveness.

5. Anti-Ragging Committee

Dedicated to preventing and addressing instances of ragging or bullying within the college campus, ensuring a zero-tolerance policy.

Objectives:

  • Prevent Ragging through strict policies and awareness.
  • Promote Respect and Dignity for all students.
  • Ensure Compliance with National and Institutional Laws.
  • Investigate Complaints of ragging impartially and promptly.
  • Provide Support to Victims including counseling.
  • Ensure Fair Punishment against offenders.
