INFRASTRUCTURE & BEYOND

Facilities & Activities

We believe that the best learning happens in an environment that is well-equipped, safe, and nurturing. Explore our world-class infrastructure and clinical ecosystem.

Classrooms & Laboratories

Our classrooms are modern, well-equipped, and designed to provide an interactive learning experience. Advanced teaching aids, including projectors and smart boards, create an environment conducive to effective learning.

Specialized laboratories for all nursing disciplines are equipped with high-fidelity mannequins and simulation tools, ensuring hands-on practical training for students to excel in clinical competencies.

Digital Library

The library is a treasure trove of nursing literature and research journals, offering comprehensive resources to support academic and professional growth. It features digital access for e-books and journals, a quiet study space, and photocopy facilities, catering to the diverse needs of students and faculty.

Clinical ecosystem

We have robust clinical partnerships with renowned hospitals, providing students with real-world exposure to patient care. These collaborations offer opportunities for hands-on training under the guidance of experienced professionals, ensuring the highest standards of clinical education.

The Community Health Nursing program emphasizes outreach and public health initiatives. Through collaborations with local health organizations and primary health centers, students actively engage in health screenings and awareness programs.
