Admission Details

Welcome to the Admission Portal of Mount Zion College of Nursing. We are excited to help you begin your journey in nursing excellence.

Eligibility Criteria

1. Academic Requirements (B.Sc. Nursing)

  • Candidates must have passed 10+2 with Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology) and English (Core/Elective).
  • Minimum aggregate of 45% marks from a recognized board (AISSCE, CBSE, ICSE, SSCE, HSCE, etc.).

2. NRI Candidates Eligibility

  • 12th-grade diploma or equivalent from a recognized board or university.
  • Minimum 50% aggregate marks in Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and English.
  • Additional Requirements for NRI:
    • Photocopy of Passport and Visa.
    • Four recent passport-sized photographs.
    • Details of residence in India.
    • HIV/AIDS/COVID-19 test report from a WHO-recognized institution.

Nationality & Requirements

  • Citizen & Nativity: Candidate should be an Indian citizen and a native of Tamil Nadu.
  • Nativity Exemption: Candidates who studied from 6th to 12th standard in Tamil Nadu schools do not need a Nativity Certificate but must provide proof of residence.

Age Limit

  • Minimum: 17 years completed by 31st December of the admission year.
Category Maximum Age (as of Dec 31st)
OC / BC / BCM / MBC / DNC 30 Years
SC / SCA / ST 35 Years

Scholarship Details

  • First Graduate Scholarship: Must submit a valid First Graduate Certificate for eligibility.
  • Government Scholarships: Eligible students can apply for Government of Tamil Nadu scholarships by submitting required documents during admission.

Selection Process

  1. Entrance Exam: Based on institutional entrance performance.
  2. Interview: Shortlisted candidates will be invited for a personal interview.
  3. Physical Fitness: Must submit a Medical Fitness Certificate from an authorized medical officer.

How to Apply

For local and NRI candidates, you can apply online or visit our administrative office.
